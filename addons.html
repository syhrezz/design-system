Mobile Patterns

Responsive breakpoints and mobile-specific overrides. Sidebar becomes an off-canvas drawer on mobile. Grid collapses. Topbar shows hamburger. Bottom navigation bar appears on mobile for the sales role.

BreakpointValueLayout Change
Desktop> 1024pxSidebar fixed visible, full grid (4-col KPI, 2-col cards)
Tablet768–1024pxSidebar collapsed to icon rail, KPI grid 2-col, cards stack
Mobile< 768pxSidebar off-canvas (translateX(-100%) → 0 on open), KPI 1-col, bottom nav bar
