Writing Guidelines

Consistent writing in the UI builds trust and reduces confusion. These guidelines cover button labels, empty state messages, error text, form labels, and toast notifications across Syhrezz.

Voice & Tone

PrincipleDescriptionExample
ClearUse plain language. Avoid jargon."Save" not "Persist changes"
SpecificName the thing being acted on."Delete User" not "Delete"
ActiveUse active voice for actions."Add Item" not "Prospect added"
ConciseUse the fewest words needed."No results found" not "There are currently no results matching your search query"
HelpfulGuide the user toward the next action."No items yet. Start building your pipeline." not just "No prospects."

Button Labels

DoDon'tReason
Save UserSubmit / OK / DoneName the entity being saved
Add ItemClick Here / Create NewDescribe the action and its object
Delete UserRemove / EraseUse "Delete" consistently for destructive actions
Export CSVDownload / Get FileSpecify the format
CancelClose / Never mind / Go backUse "Cancel" consistently for dismissing modals

Form Labels

Form labels are always: UPPERCASE, 12px, fw-700, letter-spacing:0.5px. Label text should be a noun or short noun phrase — never a question or instruction.

DoDon't
Full NameEnter your full name
Email AddressWhat is your email?
RoleSelect a role for this user
NotesAdd any additional notes here

Page Titles

Page headings (h1) follow the pattern: [Entity] [Context]. Always 24px fw-800 letter-spacing:-0.8px.

Detail page"PT Maju Jaya" (entity name directly)"Customer Detail" / "View Customer"
Settings"System Settings" / "Notification Settings""Settings" / "Configure"
